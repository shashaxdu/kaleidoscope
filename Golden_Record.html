On your journey through space, you’ve stumbled upon Voyager 1! Voyager 1 a space probe that was launched in 1977 for the purpose of studying the outer solar system. It has travelled farther than any other man-made object has from Earth.

On the Voyager lies a message-in-a-bottle for the cosmos: the Golden Record, a gold-plated disc that was created in case the spacecraft is ever found by intelligent life from planets outside our Solar System (called exoplanets). The Golden Record carries spoken greetings from the people of Earth, photos of life on Earth, music from diverse cultures, the “Sounds of Earth”, which include the sounds of whales, a baby crying, and waves crashing on the shore, among other things. The Golden Record also includes a range of scientific information written in a way that we hope extraterrestrials will understand. Lastly, it includes the brain waves of Ann Duryan, the Creative Director of the Golden Record project.

To explore the contents of the Golden Record, click on the golden disc!
