The Milky Way

.

The Milky Way is home to our Sun, as well as billions of other stars. It is 100,000 lightyears across. (Remember how fast light travels? At 186,000 miles per second, it would take light 100,000 years to travel across the Milky Way).

At the center of the Milky Way is a giant black hole. In fact, scientists believe that black holes lie at the center of most galaxies.

The Solar System. Everyone you know is here.
