Hallelujah, you’ve reached Neptune, the last planet in our Solar System! While it took you 242 light-minutes to reach Neptune (or 4 light-hours), it would take NASA about 12 years to get there.

The next planet, Jupiter, is more than 10 times as far from Earth as Mars is. Travelling at the speed of light, it would only take you 33 minutes to travel the 365 million miles between Earth and Jupiter. In comparison, it would take NASA 6 years to get there.

Travelling at the speed of light, it only took you 3 minutes to travel the 33.9 million miles between Earth and Mars. In comparison, it would take NASA astronauts about 6 months to get to Mars in a vessel suitable for humans.
