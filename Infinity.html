“There are infinite numbers between 0 and 1. There's .1 and .12 and .112 and an infinite collection of others. Of course, there is a bigger infinite set of numbers between 0 and 2, or between 0 and a million. Some infinities are bigger than other infinities.”

-John Green, The Fault in Our Stars

Beyond the Observable Universe, scientists believe that the universe just … keeps on going. Yup, astronomers suspect that the universe is actually infinite. Is that a difficult concept to wrap your head around? Well, you’re not alone. Explore how others have pondered the concept of infinity in this last segment of your intergalactic journey. When you’re done, get ready to return home. You’ve come a long, long way!
